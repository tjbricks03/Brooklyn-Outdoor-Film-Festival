About

For those about to rock...all the outdoor movies will be playing in Brooklyn Bridge Park this summer.


Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival is a summer must that has earned a large and loyal following. Since its launch in 2018, we’ve wowed, entertained, thrilled, and pulled at the heartstrings with the maximum of 5,000 moviegoers, making our program one of New York City’s favorite summer film series. The film showing will take place on August 5th to 8th. There is no better place to be on a hot summer night than watching a classic film and enjoying the breeze off of the East River from Harbor View Lawn with the dazzling Manhattan skyline before you.

News

Experience entertainment in Brooklyn's Bridge Park

Brooklyn Bridge Park Conservancy’s “Movies With A View” series will kick off again on Thursday, July 12 and this summer, it is taking female representation to the next level! The conservancy is adding SHE TALKS, a new talk series that will put leading women in the fields of media, the arts and politics center stage as they share their perspectives on their fields and New York City.

Experience entertainment in Governor's Island

Free screenings throughout the summer take place on the newly restored eight-acre parade ground during Governors Island’s Late Fridays. All screenings are free and open to the public with pre-show entertainment kicking off at 7:00pm, including a beer garden and food vendors. Films begin at dusk.

Experience entertainment on Rooftop

Rooftop Films roves around Brooklyn’s rooftops, courtyards, and now its cemeteries. Catch one at Green-Wood this summer, where there is plenty of room to spread out.
